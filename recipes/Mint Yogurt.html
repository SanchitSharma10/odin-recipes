Mint Yogurt

Description

Basic Mint Yogurt sauce used in many Salads and sides, but also famous for Biryani

Ingredients

  • Mint
  • Yogurt
  • Sugar
  • Salt
  • Garlic
  • Lemon Juice
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Mix all ingredients together rigoursly. Adjust for salt and lemon juice. Garnish with more mint. Enjoy!
