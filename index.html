kadai-chicken

Description

Indian chicken recipe cooked in a kadai(open pan) with traditional indian spices like kashmiri red chilli, cloves, green chillies and much more.

Ingredients

  • chicken
  • garlic
  • ginger
  • tomato
  • green chilli
  • indian spices

Steps

  1. Cook garlic, ginger and tomatoes in a hot pan
  2. Once they form a thick gravy, add the chicken
  3. Fully cook the chicken in the gravy and add salt as nescessary
  4. Add green chilli and then some water and cover it on medium heat for 15 minutes
  5. Serve

Biryani

Description

Famous around the world for its succulent taste, the chicken biryani is more akin to a rice rish moreso than its meat counterpart. Enjoyed around the world it has many variations spanning from middle-east to india, with this recipe being from Hyderabad

Ingredients

  • Chicken Thigh and Leg Bone in
  • Yogurt
  • Ginger
  • Garlic
  • Green Cardamom
  • Black Cardamom
  • Cinnamon
  • Bay Leaf
  • Biryani Masala or Garam Masala
  • Lemon

Steps

  1. Mix all the ingredients in a large bowl, a long with some lemon juice
  2. Marinate this mixture in the fridge for about 4 hours and preferably overnight
  3. Boil rice in another pan until it is about 75% cooked, a long with some cardamom and clove
  4. Take another utensil that is preferably tall, and layer chicken mixture with rice on top, and continue this in layers until finished.
  5. On the top layer, add some optional fried onion, and desi ghee and optional rose water with coriander leaf
  6. Close this utensil and cook it on medium heat for 15min, and low for 5. Turn off flame after done, wait 2 minutes and open and serve.

hakka

Description

A unique recipe that was initally made by the chinese in Calutta India that hailed from the Hakka in China. They combined indian tastes with chinese ingredients and came up with a dish that is famous mostly in the North American Continent and is an excellent dish paired with steamed/fried rice.

Ingredients

  • chicken
  • green chilli
  • Soy sauce
  • chicken broth
  • garlic
  • ginger
  • onion
  • green onion
  • egg
  • corn starch
  • black pepper
  • green or red chilli sauce
  • Coriander leaves with stems

Steps

  1. First deep fry bite-sized chicken mixed with cornstarch and egg in a hot wok with oil and then drain
  2. Take oil again in the wok and add ginger garlic and stir it around until its aroma is present
  3. Now add the white onion and green chilli. Mix the red chilli sauce in there and add 1-2 tbsp of soy-sauce as well. Cook this for about 1-2 minutes.
  4. Take some water/broth and add it to the wok. Let it cook on medium to low flame for about 5 minutes. Optional you can also add coriander stems as well.
  5. Add corn starch to the mixture and let it come to a boil. Once the sauce thicken, turn off flame and add the deep-fried chicken. Garnish with coriander and green onions. Serve with steamed rice.

lasgana

Description

A famous dish that has laeyed pasta noodles with cheeses, tomato sauce, sometimes meat and veggies too.

Ingredients

  • Lasgana pasta noodle strips
  • Spinach optional
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Ricotta Cheese
  • Cottage cheese
  • tomato sauce

Steps

  1. Boil the pasta until it is near al-dente. Also if you are using spinach cook that in a different pan until its ready (can add tomato and onions for more flavor)
  2. Next take the noodle strips and add it to an oven tray.
  3. Every layer, repeat and add mozarella, your choice of veggie/meat, tomato sauce, and any cheeses you wish, and then add another layer of pasta noodles ontop of it
  4. Turn the oven on and heat it for 350-375F for 25 minutes
  5. Serve

pizza

Description

Pizza perhaps the most famous amongst foods needs no introduction. Here is a recipe that helps make margherita pizza

Ingredients

  • Italian Tipo 00 Flour or Bread flour
  • Yeast
  • Sugar
  • Salt
  • Tomato sauce
  • Basil (optional)
  • Mozzarella
  • Olive oil

Steps

  1. First take whatever flour you have, and mix it with water. 75% hydration by weight, and mix in salt, sugar, active or instant dry yeast as well as some olive oil.
  2. Once this forms a dough ball you, can either knead it for 1-2 hours and put it away in a cold place, or straight away cover the dough, and put it in a cold area. The first method will allow you rise the dough under 14 hours whereas the second method will take atleast 14 hours if not more to rise the dough without knead.
  3. Once your dough ball has risen, take a piece and stretch it with hands onto the surace. Lift it once or twice in the air to let air into the dough so that it doesn't tear as much when forming the pizza
  4. Turn on ur stove and broiler. On the stove preheat your cast iron for atleast 5-10 minutes on high flame. When ready place the pizza dough you formed onto the cast iron. Quickly spread tomato sauce all over the exposed part of the surface, a long with mozzarella and optional basil if you have. Do a quick drip of oliveoil around the the pizza and pop it into the broiler as close as you can get it safely.
  5. With stove off now and only broiler on, keep moving the cast iron handle (using oven mitts) from side to ensure even cooking. Take a look every so often to make sure the pizza does not burn on the top. It will be done when it looks like the image provided above. Serve and cut into 4 pieces and enjoy!

shawarma

Description

Another famous recipe that really needs no introduction. Below are the steps to make succulent Shawarma!

Ingredients

  • Your choice of veggies, but atleast Lettuce and Onion
  • Sumac
  • Pita
  • Chicken
  • Chicken Shawarma Spices:
    • Cumin
    • Cinnamon
    • Black Pepper
    • Coiander
    • Paprika
    • Cardamom
  • Garlic Sauce

Steps

  1. Marinate Chicken with the spices listed. Cook them on an open flame or broil/roast them, and then cut into strips and put aside
  2. Warm pita on the stove, then open it using a butter knife and with the same knife spread the garlic sauce and sumac on the insides. Add chicken and vegetables and any other toppings desired into this pocket area
  3. Wrap up the outer side of the shawarma until it looks like a roll and then put it back onto the stove on a flat pan. Add a counterweight on the top to give it a press grilled look and grill it for about 1minute on each side.
  4. Remove the shawarma and add it to a plate and cut it down the middle
  5. Enjoy!
