Garlic Sauce

Description

Basic Garlic Sauce used for Shawarma and many more. Tasty!

Ingredients

  • Garlic
  • Oil
  • Lemon Juice
  • Salt

Steps

  1. carefully blend garlic and salt with 25% of the oil in a food blender first.
  2. Add the lemon juice, and slowly keep adding more oil as the blender is active
  3. Scrape the sides every so often and blend until desired consistency. Enjoy!
